Who we are

Meet the Coalition

We formed Oregon Heals in 2021, in the wake of a public health crisis that hit our communities hardest — youth, caregivers, and Oregonians of color carrying grief and trauma the existing system was never built to hold.

The current mental health system is not adequately serving our communities. Black, Indigenous, and other people of color often have the least access to care and face the most stigma when they try to find it. We're here to change that — through policy, advocacy, and the steady work of building something better together.

Our Vision

Vision Statement

"A robust mental health system in Oregon that is equitable, affordable, culturally responsive, community centered and community driven. A holistic system where our communities can process pain, grief, and trauma — and live our lives to our fullest capacity."
